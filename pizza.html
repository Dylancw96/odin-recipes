Pizza

Description

Pizza is... Well lets be honest, you know what pizza is. Its a flat piece of bread that is topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and a variety of toppings. Pizza is a staple of American culture and is a favorite of many.

Pizza is a very versatile food that can be made in many different ways. You can have a thin crust pizza, a deep dish pizza, a stuffed crust pizza, and many more. You can have a pizza with just cheese, or you can have a pizza with every topping imaginable. Pizza is a food that can be made to fit anyones tastes.

Ingredients

  • 1.25 cups all-purposes flour
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • .75 tsp of instant yeast
  • .5 tsp kosher salt
  • .25 cup plus 3 tablespoons cold water
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Cornmeal for dusting
  • 1 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup toppings of your choice

Directions

  1. Combine flour, sugar, yeast and salt and mix well
  2. Lightly oil work surface, set dough ball on top: knead until smooth, about 1 minute. Cover with plastic wrap and let rest 15 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F. If you have a pizza stone, place it on the bottom rack of the oven.
  4. Divide dough into two equal pieces. Roll each piece of dough into a 10-inch
  5. Transfer dough to a pizza peel or rimless cookie sheet dusted with cornmeal.
  6. Top with sauce, cheese, and toppings of your choice.
  7. Transfer pizza from peel to oven or slide pizza onto preheated pizza stone. Bake pizza until cheese and crust are golden brown, about 10 minutes. Transfer to a cutting board and slice. Serve immediately.
  8. Enjoy!
