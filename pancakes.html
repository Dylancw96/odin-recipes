Pancakes

Pancakes are notibly one of the best and perhaps the best breakfast item out there. They are a flat cake that is made from a batter and cooked on a griddle. The can be topped with syrup, fruit, whipped cream, etc.

Perfect pancakes are easier to make than you think. This pancake recipes produces pancakes that are light and fluffy and delicious with just a few simple Ingredients

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, or vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • Vegetable oil or melted butter for cooking

Directions

  1. Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a medium bowl to combine.
  2. Whisk milk, melted butter, and egg in a medium bowl until combined.
  3. Pour milk mixture into flour mixture and whisk until just combined (batter will be thick and lumpy).
  4. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium. Lightly coat skillet with oil. Working in batches, pour 1/4-cupfuls batter onto skillet. Cook pancakes until bottoms are golden brown and bubbles form on top, about 3 minutes. Flip and cook until cooked through and other side of pancakes are golden brown, about 2 minutes longer. Serve pancakes with butter and syrup.
  5. Enjoy!
